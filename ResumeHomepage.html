RESUME

Definition:

A resume is a concise document summarizing an individual's education, work experience, skills, and accomplishments

Why is it helpful?

  • Provides a snapshot of qualifications and suitability for a job
  • Helps employers quickly assess a candidate's background and determine if they're a good fit for a position

How is it used?

  • Integral part of job applications
  • Outline for conversation in job interviews
  • Handed out at career fairs and other hiring events
